OM SAI SRI SAI JAYA JAYA SAI

"whatever you do, wherever you may be, always bear this in mind. I am aware of everything you do." - Shri Shirdi Sai Baba

      	Shradha 		    	        	Saburi
 			"Tvamev mata cha pita twamev, Tvamev banducha sakha tvamev
			  Tvamev vidya dravinam tvamev, Tvamev sarva mam dev dev"
  • Sayings of Shirdi Sai Baba
  • "Whoever puts his feet on Shirdi Soil, his sufferings would come to an end."
  • "The wretched and miserable will rise to Joy and Happiness as soon as they climb the steps of the Mosque."
  • "I shall ever be Active and Vigorous even after leaving this earthly body."
  • "My Tomb shall Bless and Speak to the needs of My devotees."
  • "I shall ever be Active and Vigorous even from My Tomb."
  • " My mortal remains will speak from My Tomb."
  • "I am ever living to guide and help those who come to Me, who surrender to Me and who seek refuge in Me.
  • "If you look to Me, I WILL look for you."
  • "If you cast your burden on Me, I shall surely bear it."
  • "If you seek My advice and help, it shall be given to you at once."
  • "There shall never be any want in the house of My devotees."
